FAQ

How do I explore schools?

Simply select the board (CBSE, SSC, or ICSE) and start exploring the available schools.

Can I access school websites directly?

Yes, each school card has an 'Explore' button that takes you to the school's website.

Are the listings regularly updated?

Absolutely! We ensure that our school listings are regularly updated to provide accurate information.

Do you offer personalized recommendations?

Yes, our Premium and Ultimate plans include personalized school recommendations based on your preferences.

How can I get priority support?

Priority support is included in our Ultimate plan, ensuring that your queries are addressed promptly.
